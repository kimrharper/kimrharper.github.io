After completing my MA in TESOL (pedagogy and linguistics), I decided to apply my background to data science. I approached language as a social phenomenon and developed curricula that emphasized natural learning

Now I use data science in the same that I approached second language learning while adhering to modern concepts in cognitive linguistics.

I have employed research skills acquired from the MA program to ensure that the data science research I conduct, is significant and repeatable. Conclusions are quantifiable and broken down into marketable results.

I spent the past 10 years working in education and have recently decided to apply my background in linguistics to that of data science.

My most recent completed project is an LSTM neural network that predicts the L1 of students based on their L2 English writing patterns.
